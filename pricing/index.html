Pricing

Every project is different, so pricing is set per project. We work with both fixed-price quotes and hourly rates, whichever fits the job better.

The rates below are hourly, excluding VAT. They reflect a simple preference: we like working closely with rural communities and small businesses, and our pricing favors that work.

Open source

Open source discount
10 %

If the work can be released under one of our approved open source licenses, we offer a discount. Open source software has a way of taking on a life of its own: other developers can find it, use it, improve it, and contribute fixes or features back. Even when that doesn't happen, you're left with software that isn't locked to any single vendor - anyone with the right skills can maintain or extend it. Ask us about which licenses we work with.

Community work

We care about the communities we're part of. For charities, community projects, and similar non-profit work, we may take the job on at a reduced rate or at no charge. Just ask.
